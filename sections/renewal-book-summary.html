Operating Cost Component Definitions:

Fronting Fees (4.65%)
Payment to AIG for issuing policies on paper and providing their financial rating. Since the captive isn't licensed in all states, AIG "fronts" the policies, allowing you to operate nationwide.
Taxes (4.10%)
State premium taxes required by each jurisdiction where you operate. These vary by state but average 4.10% across the captive.
Brokerage (4.00%)
Compensation to your insurance broker (AssuredPartners) for program administration, renewal negotiations, and ongoing service throughout the year.
Claims Administration (4.00%)
Sedgwick handles all claims from first notice through settlement. Includes 24/7 claims reporting and dedicated adjusters.
Loss Control (1.00%)
Safety and risk management services including site inspections, safety training, fleet management programs, and ergonomic assessments to help prevent losses.
Offshore Expense (5.75%)
Captive management fees covering Bermuda operations, regulatory compliance, financial reporting, board meetings, actuarial services, audits, and day-to-day captive administration.
